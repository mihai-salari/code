Description

This is a simple password generator run on your Apple Watch, it can generate a password of 8 characters.

User can choose to have the special characters (!@#$%) in the generated password or without those special characters by tap on the 'special characters' button. A dimmed button means the 'special characters' is off, vice versa.

To get a new password, just tap on the password screen and new password will be generated everytime you tap on the screen.
